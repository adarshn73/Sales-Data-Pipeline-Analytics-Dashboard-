Project Title:
Sales Forecasting & Interactive Analytics Dashboard for a Supplement Brand

Company:
PulseFuel Nutrition

Description:
This project simulates a pre-workout supplement business inspired by real-world brands. We generated synthetic historical sales data, cleaned and analyzed it in Python, created KPI summaries and forecasts, and built a Power BI dashboard to visualize business performance.

Main KPIs:
- Revenue
- Profit
- Units Sold
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Profit Margin

Business Questions:
- Which products generate the most revenue?
- Which products generate the most profit?
- Which channel performs best?
- Which region performs best?
- Are there seasonal patterns in demand?
- What does the next 3 months of sales look like?
